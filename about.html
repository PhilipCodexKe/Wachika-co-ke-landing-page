About Us...

Who We Are

Wachikart Limited based in Nairobi runs a digital platform Wachikart dedicated to offering effective marketing and promotional solutions for shops and business services.

With a mission to improve the quality of life through access to better products and services. The company places core values like entrepreneurship, quality life and technology at the forefront of its operations.

Wachikart's B2B, B2C & C2C online shopping marketplace opens door for better products and services access. Our directories offers detailed product profiles, fostering informed consumer choices and facilitating partnership between businesses and potential customers. as well as professionals with potential clients.

Company Goals

Establishing a retail ecosystem: bridging online and offline shopping experiences, enhancing merchant's sales and marketing productivity

Global Expansion: Extending our services to millions worldwide, creating job opportunities and venturing into new markets through acquisitions.

Our Team

At Wachikart, our success story is built on dedication and passion of our diverse team, united by a common goal of transforming E-commerce and revolutionizing the business landscape, our professionals bring a wealth of experience, creativity and innovation to the table

Our team' s diversity is our strength. Hailing from various professional backgrounds, we foster an environment of collaboration and open communication.

Our Core Purpose

To enhance the quality of life by allowing access to superior products and services through effective marketing and promotions.

To set industry standards for professionalism, integrity and reliability in digital marketing solutions.

Our Core Values

Entrepreneurship: We recognize the integral role of sellers, affiliates, customers and clients in providing and accessing quality products and services.


Technology: We harness the power of technology to ensure convenient access to high quality products and services.


Quality: Our commitment to marketing and promotion is rooted in the belief that it enhances the overall quality of products and services.


Life: By facilitating access to better products and services, we contribute to improving the quality of life for both individuals and communities at large.
