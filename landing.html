What is Wachikart?

Wachikart Limited is a digital platform company offering marketing and promotional solutions for businesses. It provides tools to automate advertising, marketing, automated communication and customer care. We offer online services through Online Shops, Online Shopping, Secure Payments, Order Fulfillment, Digital Marketing, SMS and Email Campaigns, Book Keeping & Accounts, Whatsapp Automation, Web Development, Hosting and Domain names.

Online Shops Features
